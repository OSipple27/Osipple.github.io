Calculate Your Cost

Everyone knows weddings are "costly" when you include everything from the venue to the guestbook. Some of the biggest ticket items are the venue, catering, floral, decor, and a wedding planner if you select that option. During our wedding planning process, there were also costs we didn't consider like liablity insurance! Why is this a thing? Well lets say if a bridesmaid sprains an ankle while being escorted by a groomsmen or if that drunk uncle one again drinks a bit too much. No matter what it is because it's your event, the consequenes are on you if something breaks or someone is injured. While trying to keep all those costs in mind it's easy to forget about the cost of suits, ties, and various accesories.
